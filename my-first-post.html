Automation vs. Art: The AI Music Dilemma

I'm interested in whether AI will ever reach a point where mixing, cloning, and sound and lyric production will be automatic based on prompts. While the prompt-based format is great for making music, AI can also copy people's work by using other artists' voices to create different songs.

The backlash against AI use in music is negative, and I'm curious whether studios like Udio, ElevenLabs, and Suno will be regulated to be legal for commercial use, or if they'll be shut down due to copyright infringement.

"The intersection of generative convenience and creative ownership is becoming the next great legal battlefield."
